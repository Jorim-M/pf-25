The Arduino Pet Pot Plant is a whimsical and interactive prototype designed to simulate the experience of caring for an artificial pet plant. Combining hardware and software, the project uses an Arduino Adafruit board to create a tactile, light-reactive flower coupled with a retro-inspired digital display powered by Processing.

‍

This playful prototype aims to merge technology and emotional engagement, offering users a fun and interactive experience while experimenting with creative IoT concepts.

By turning an artificial plant into an emotional companion, this project demonstrates the potential of IoT and gamification in interaction design.

‍

The project’s focus in engaging users opens up exciting possibilities for future explorations in playful, technology-driven design.
